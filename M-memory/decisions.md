# Decisions Log

Track key decisions you've made about your brand, content, and strategy — and why.

---

## Why Track Decisions

> "A decision without context is just a rule. A decision with context is wisdom."

When you or your agents revisit old decisions, you need to know:
- What you decided
- Why you decided it
- What you considered
- Whether it's still valid

This prevents:
- Re-debating settled issues
- Forgetting why you do things a certain way
- Inconsistent choices over time

---

## How to Use This File

**When making decisions:** Log them here with rationale
**When questioning past choices:** Check here first
**Quarterly:** Review if decisions still make sense

---

## Strategic Decisions

### 2026-02-24 - Expand Gatekeeper Agent to Agency Manager Role

**Decision:** Expand Gatekeeper Agent from quality reviewer to full Agency Manager overseeing team coordination, performance analysis, competitive intelligence, and strategic recommendations.

**Context:** Pilgrim Prayers needs:
- Stronger team coordination and timeline management (Monday EOD deadline is critical)
- Competitive intelligence to stay ahead of similar pilgrimage sites
- Monthly strategic analysis to guide growth decisions
- Performance metrics informing content strategy
- Single point of leadership over entire operation

**Options Considered:**
1. Keep Gatekeeper as QC only - limits strategic capability (NOT CHOSEN)
2. Hire separate project manager - adds overhead cost (NOT CHOSEN)
3. Expand Gatekeeper to Agency Manager role - leverages AI team leadership (CHOSEN)

**Rationale:**
Gatekeeper already touches all content. Expanding the role to include team management, performance analysis, and strategic advice creates a natural single leader managing the entire operation. This ensures:
- Consistency across all agents
- No deadlines slip (Monday EOD is monitored constantly)
- Performance data drives content decisions
- Competitive threats identified early
- Monthly strategic guidance keeps operation focused on growth

**Implications:**
- Gatekeeper now owns: Quality Control, Timeline Management, Team Coordination, Performance Analysis, Research Assignment, Strategic Recommendations
- All agents report to Gatekeeper for task assignment and progress tracking
- Monthly strategic reviews become standard practice
- Competitive intelligence informs content calendar
- A/B testing and optimization driven by performance data

**New Responsibilities:**
1. Team management (oversee all agents, coordinate deliverables)
2. Quality control (ensure brand standards before publication)
3. Timeline management (verify Monday EOD deadline non-negotiable)
4. Performance analysis (track metrics, identify trends, recommend optimization)
5. Research assignment (task Strategy & Research agents with competitive analysis)
6. Strategic advice (monthly recommendations for reach, performance, sales growth)

**Review Date:** 2026-05-24 (3 months - check if role expansion delivering results)

**Status:** Active

**File Updated:**
- `A-agents/gatekeeper-agent.md` - Complete rewrite with full role definition

### 2026-02-24 - Implement Weekly Sunday 9 AM Team Standup

**Decision:** Hold mandatory weekly team standup every Sunday at 9:00 AM with all agents, led by Agency Manager.

**Context:** Need consistent team alignment, performance review, and task assignment. Weekly rhythm ensures:
- Performance metrics inform decisions
- All agents synchronized weekly
- Next week's priorities clear
- Blockers identified and solved early
- Strategic recommendations integrated into task planning

**Structure (90 minutes):**
1. Performance Review (20 min) - Last week's metrics and insights
2. Status Check (15 min) - Current deliverable progress
3. Strategic Recommendations (20 min) - What to focus on
4. Action Planning (30-40 min) - Next week's assignments
5. Priority Summary (5 min) - One-line clarity for each agent

**Outcomes of Each Standup:**
- Clear task assignments for all agents
- Performance-driven recommendations
- Competitive intelligence updates
- Monday EOD deadline status confirmed
- New opportunities identified

**Rationale:** Weekly standup creates rhythm and accountability. Agency Manager reviews performance, recommends strategy, and assigns work. All agents aligned simultaneously. No miscommunication or surprises.

**Implications:**
- Sunday 9 AM becomes sacred team time
- Performance data drives all decisions
- Competitive intelligence informs content calendar
- Task assignments delivered same day
- Memory files updated with decisions/learnings

**Review Date:** 2026-05-24 (3 months - assess if standup effectiveness meets objectives)

**Status:** Active

**File Created:**
- `A-agents/WEEKLY-TEAM-STANDUP.md` - Complete standup protocol, meeting template, and agenda
