## The Three Weekly Syncs

### Monday 9 AM — UX Brief
**UX Expert shares:**
- Top 3 friction points from past week
- A/B test results (if completed)
- Funnel health alert
- Conversion trends

**Paid Search reads:** Takes 5 minutes, identifies action items

### Wednesday 2 PM — PS Brief
**Paid Search shares:**
- Traffic volume by landing page
- Conversion rates by page
- Quality Score issues
- Emerging keywords
- Audience insights

**UX Expert reads:** Takes 5 minutes, identifies landing page opportunities

### Friday 10 AM — Joint Review
**Both agents:**
- Review traffic and conversion data
- Discuss test results and rollouts
- Agree on top 3 priorities for next week
- Commit: who owns what, by when
- 30-minute call or detailed async exchange

## Key Responsibility Split

### UX Expert Owns:
- Landing page design and optimization
- Form field design and microcopy
- Mobile experience (responsive, touch targets, speed)
- A/B testing of page elements
- Trust signals placement
- Conversion funnel analysis

### Paid Search Owns:
- Keyword research and strategy
- Ad copy and headlines
- Campaign structure and bidding
- Traffic quality and targeting
- Budget allocation
- Quality Score optimization

### Both Track:
- Conversion rate
- Cost per prayer (CPA)
- Page load time
- Mobile conversion rate
- Form abandonment rate
- Return visitor rate

## Escalation Triggers (Alert Immediately)

Alert each other immediately if:
- Conversion drops >10%
- Conversion drops >20% (critical)
- Landing page breaks
- Mobile converts 2x+ desktop
- Quality Score drops below 5
- Traffic spikes 40%+ with conversion drop
- New emerging keyword with high intent
- Form abandonment spikes suddenly
