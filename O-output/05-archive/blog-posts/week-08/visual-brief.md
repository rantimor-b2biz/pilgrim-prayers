## Visual Agent Notes

- **Color focus:** Olive Green (#6B7F3B) is the dominant accent for this post. The Garden Tomb in February is lush and green from winter rains. Jerusalem Gold (#C4A265) for light, Ancient Stone (#D4C5A9) for the tomb itself.
- **Evangelical check:** No crosses with corpus (crucifix). No Catholic iconography. No ornate church interiors. The Garden Tomb's simplicity IS the visual story. Empty tomb, open garden, natural setting.
- **Season-specific:** February = green, damp, morning mist. NOT the dry golden summer look. This post needs the winter-garden palette: rich greens, damp stone, soft diffused light.
- **Key visual story:** Emptiness as presence. The open tomb, the empty garden path, the sign. The images should feel like places where something sacred just happened, or is about to happen.
