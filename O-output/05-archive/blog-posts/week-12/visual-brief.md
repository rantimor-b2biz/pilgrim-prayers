## Hero Image

## Visual Agent Notes

### Why This Approach
The post's core idea is "this is a living land, not a museum." The visuals must reinforce this: no postcard-perfect shots, no tourist angles. Every image should feel like it was taken by someone who lives there,warm, real, present. The progression from wide (hero) to agricultural (Degania) to vast (Golan) to personal (Uri) mirrors the post's journey from wonder to daily life to personal connection.

### Color Focus
- **Primary:** Jerusalem Gold (#C4A265),morning light
- **Secondary:** Olive Green (#6B7F3B),Galilee hills and fields
- **Accent:** Sky Blue (#87CEEB),water and sky
- **Grounding:** Deep Earth (#5C4033),basalt stones, soil

### Evangelical Check
- [x] No Catholic/Orthodox iconography
- [x] No crosses, churches, or religious buildings in frame
- [x] Focus on nature, land, and water,universal to all Christians
- [x] No political elements (no flags, barriers, military)
- [x] The Golan Heights is shown as landscape, not a political statement

### Alternatives Considered
- Close-up of hands touching Galilee water → Saved for a future "quiet moment" post
- Fishing nets on the shore → Could feel too staged/symbolic
- Aerial drone shot → Too distant and cold for this post's intimate "living land" feel
