# Week 8 Content Brief: "A Quiet Morning at the Garden Tomb"

**Calendar Week:** February 23
**Post Type:** 🕯️ QUIET (The Quiet Moment)
**Holy Land Site:** Garden Tomb
**Theme:** Stillness; listening for God
**Special Element:** Embedded video of quiet paths inside the Garden

---

## Executive Summary

This week we're creating an immersive, contemplative piece centered on the Garden Tomb. Rather than teaching or preaching, we're inviting readers into a moment of stillness — both through prose and through video footage of the peaceful, quiet paths inside the garden. The post should feel like a meditation, a pause, an invitation to listen.

---

## Brand Alignment

**Voice:** Warm, calm, grounded in faith (NOT preachy)
**Tone:** Contemplative, gentle, meditative
**Audience:** Believers seeking spiritual peace, connection, and the presence of God
**Promise:** "You can feel closer to sacred places, even from far away"

**What we're NOT doing:**
- ✗ Theological lecture
- ✗ Detailed history lesson
- ✗ Emotional manipulation or guilt
- ✗ "Act now" pressure
- ✗ Flowery, overly sentimental language

**What we ARE doing:**
- ✓ Inviting readers into a sensory experience
- ✓ Creating space for reflection
- ✓ Showing the sacredness of the place
- ✓ Ending with an open question (not a demand)

### ✍️ COPYWRITER AGENT — Brief & Deliverable

**Your job:** Write the blog post that weaves research, story, sensory detail, and the video into a cohesive, contemplative piece.

**Post structure (QUIET format):**

1. **Opening (50 words):**
   - Lead with sensory image
   - No announcement ("Today we're talking about...")
   - Example: "Early morning light enters the Garden Tomb. The stone is cool. The birds are singing. And for a moment, you hear nothing but God."

2. **The Story (150 words):**
   - Integrate Storyteller's narrative
   - Keep it intimate, personal
   - Let the story speak without overexplaining

3. **Video Integration (50 words):**
   - Introduce the video
   - Frame it: "Watch these quiet paths. Listen to the silence. This is where faith goes to listen."
   - Let readers know what they'll experience

4. **Reflection Section (100 words):**
   - What does stillness teach us?
   - Minimal theology (just enough to ground it spiritually)
   - Use Scripture sparingly: "Psalm 46:10 says 'Be still and know that I am God'"

5. **The Quiet Question (30 words):**
   - End with an open question (not a CTA)
   - "When you stop talking, what does God say to you?"

**Total: 300-380 words** (blog post standard)

**Tone guidelines:**
- Short sentences for contemplative pace
- Active voice, strong verbs
- Sensory language: light, stone, silence, breathing
- NO: "Amazing," "incredible," "life-changing" (let reader feel it)
- YES: Specific details, textures, moments

**Video placement:**
- Embed after the story section
- Caption: "The quiet paths of the Garden Tomb"
- Let it breathe — don't surround with too much text
