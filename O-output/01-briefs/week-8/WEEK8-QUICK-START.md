## 📝 Post Structure (🕯️ QUIET Format)

```
OPENING (50 words)
↓ Sensory detail, inviting
"Early morning light enters the Garden Tomb. The stone is cool..."

STORY (150 words)
↓ Prayer narrative of listener experiencing stillness
[Storyteller's narrative here]

VIDEO (50 words)
↓ Introduction to embedded video
"Watch these quiet paths. Listen to the silence."
[EMBEDDED VIDEO]

REFLECTION (100 words)
↓ What stillness teaches (minimal theology)
"Psalm 46:10 says 'Be still and know that I am God'"

CLOSING QUESTION (30 words)
↓ Open invitation
"When you stop talking, what does God say to you?"
```

## ✨ Tone Guide

**DO ✓**
- Warm, calm, grounded
- Sensory and specific
- Faithful but not preachy
- Inviting, not demanding

**DON'T ✗**
- Marketing hype ("Amazing!")
- Theological lecture
- Guilt or fear language
- "Act now" urgency
- False sentimentality
