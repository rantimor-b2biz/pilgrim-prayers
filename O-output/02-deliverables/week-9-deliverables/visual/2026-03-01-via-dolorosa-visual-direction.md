# Visual Direction: Via Dolorosa - Week 9
**Date:** 2026-03-01
**Agent:** Visual Agent
**For:** Digital team / Video production

---

## Core Visual Concept

**The feeling to create:** Standing on the threshold of something ancient and real. Not a museum. Not a postcard. A living street where people still bring what they cannot carry alone.

**Mood board in words:**
Cold stone. Morning light. One person. Silence in the middle of a city.

---

## Hero Image (Blog Post Thumbnail)

**Shot:** Low angle looking up the Via Dolorosa. The stone street rises ahead. Morning. The stones are wet and dark from the night's cold. The light is golden-gray, coming from the far end of the alley.

**No people in frame** (or a single small silhouette at the far end, facing away, walking upward)

**Color palette:**
- Jerusalem Stone: warm gray-beige (#C4A8A0)
- Morning shadow: deep cool gray (#4A4A52)
- Morning light shard: gold (#C4A265)
- Wet stone: dark charcoal (#2E2E35)

**Avoid:** Bright tourist photography. Crowds. Daylight clarity. Stock photo poses.
**Seek:** The weight of quiet. The texture of old stone. The sense of something that has absorbed a great deal.

---

## Video Direction (45-60 seconds)

**Opening shot (0-5s):**
Extreme close-up: the texture of stone pavement. Water in the cracks. Cold morning. The camera is at ground level.

**Shot 2 (5-15s):**
A pair of feet in simple shoes, moving slowly up the stone path. Only feet. No face. The sound: soft footsteps on stone.

**Shot 3 (15-25s):**
A hand pressed flat against a stone wall. The wall is old, uneven. The hand stays still. Nothing moves.

**Shot 4 (25-40s):**
Pull back slowly to reveal the narrow alley. The walls rise on both sides. The sky is a thin strip above. The light is cool and gray. A wooden cross leans against one wall (out of focus, background).

**Shot 5 (40-50s):**
Close-up: a folded piece of paper, white, pressed into a crack in the stone wall. A hand releases it gently. The paper stays.

**Closing shot (50-60s):**
The alley, empty again. The paper visible in the crack. The morning continuing around it.

**No music required.** If sound is added: ambient - distant city sounds, faint footsteps, the sound of cold air. No worship music. No narration.

**Text overlay (if used):**
- Font: simple serif, white, small
- Words: "The road that still receives."
- Timing: Final 10 seconds
- No animation - static text, fade in only

## Color and Tone Reference

**What we are NOT doing:**
- Bright, sunlit Jerusalem tourist imagery
- Golden hour warmth
- Inspirational poster aesthetic
- Cross-with-sunbeams stock imagery

**What we ARE doing:**
- The weight of morning
- Cold stone that holds centuries
- One person's act of placing something and walking away
- The beauty inside something serious
