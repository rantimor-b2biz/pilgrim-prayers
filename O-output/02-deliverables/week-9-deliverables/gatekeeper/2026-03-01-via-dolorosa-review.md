## Patterns Logged for Memory

1. **"Prayers travel farther than feet"** - new reusable line. Works across post, newsletter, and potentially ads.
2. **Character naming works:** Carol (not "a woman from Tennessee") - giving her a name creates instant intimacy without backstory overload.
3. **Three-word prayers are powerful:** "Carry him, Lord" - the shorter the prayer in the post, the more weight it carries. Pattern: keep character prayers to 1-5 words maximum.
4. **GSC data -> content angle:** The healing prayer audience (cancer queries dominant) was specifically targeted through Carol's story. First deliberate use of digital data to inform emotional angle.
5. **Lent timing alignment confirmed:** Structure 3 (Prayer in Place) is the right format for seasons of carrying - Lent, Advent, times of personal grief.
