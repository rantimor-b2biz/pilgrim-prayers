## 📋 Folder Purposes

### **01-briefs/**
**Purpose:** Content briefs and planning documents before production starts

**What goes here:**
- Weekly content briefs for agents
- Workflow diagrams
- Timeline documents
- Quick start guides
- Reference materials

**Examples:**
- `week-8/2026-02-24-week8-garden-tomb-brief.md` — Full brief for Week 8
- `week-8/WEEK8-QUICK-START.md` — One-page reference

**Duration:** Briefs stay here permanently for reference

---

### **02-deliverables/**
**Purpose:** Work-in-progress deliverables from agents

**What goes here:**
- Research Agent's research documents
- Storyteller's narrative briefs
- Copywriter's blog post drafts
- Gatekeeper's review notes
- Visual's direction documents
- Newsletter's email drafts

**Structure:** Organized by week and agent type

**Examples:**
- `week-8-deliverables/research/2026-02-24-garden-tomb-research.md`
- `week-8-deliverables/copywriter/2026-02-24-garden-tomb-post.md`

**Duration:** Stays here while being worked on, moves to 03-published when final

---

### **03-published/**
**Purpose:** Final published content

**What goes here:**
- Blog posts (published on website)
- Final versions after Gatekeeper approval
- Dated by month and week

**Structure:**
```
03-published/blog-posts/2026-02/week-8/
    └── garden-tomb-quiet-morning.md
```

**Examples:**
- `blog-posts/2026-02/week-8/garden-tomb-quiet-morning.md` — Published blog post

**Duration:** Permanent archive

---

### **04-campaigns/**
**Purpose:** Marketing campaigns (email, social, etc.)

**What goes here:**
- Email campaigns (finalized versions)
- Social media content calendars
- Campaign performance reports
- Email sequences

**Structure:**
```
04-campaigns/email/2026-02/
    └── week-8-garden-tomb.md
```

**Examples:**
- `email/2026-02/week-8-garden-tomb.md` — Email campaign for Week 8 post

**Duration:** Permanent archive

---

### **05-archive/**
**Purpose:** Previous work and old posts

**What goes here:**
- Old blog posts
- Previous projects
- Historical content
- Legacy folders

**Examples:**
- `old-posts/08-weekly-post-garden-tomb-quiet-morning/`
- `old-posts/12.2-weekly-post-galilee-living-land/`

**Duration:** Keep for reference, don't delete

---

### **06-ads/**
**Purpose:** Paid search and advertising

**What goes here:**
- Google Ads campaigns
- Ad copy versions
- Campaign performance data
- Keyword research
- Display ad specs

**Examples:**
- `search-campaigns/[campaign-name]/`

**Duration:** Permanent archive

## 📝 Creating New Content

**When starting a new week:**

1. Create folder: `01-briefs/week-[X]/`
2. Write main brief: `01-briefs/week-[X]/[DATE]-week-[X]-brief.md`
3. Write agent briefs: `01-briefs/week-[X]/[DATE]-[agent]-brief.md`
4. Create deliverable folders: `02-deliverables/week-[X]-deliverables/[agent]/`
5. After completion, move to `03-published/`
