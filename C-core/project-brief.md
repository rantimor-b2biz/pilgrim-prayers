# Project Brief

> Pilgrim Prayers — Personal prayer placement at holy sites in Jerusalem and the Holy Land.

---

## About Pilgrim Prayers

**What we do:** Pilgrim Prayers enables believers from around the world to place personal prayers at holy sites in Jerusalem and the Holy Land, even when they cannot be there themselves.

**Who we serve:** Bible-believing Christians, ages 35–75, based in the US, Europe, South America, and Southeast Asia — often unable to travel due to health, finances, distance, or life circumstances.

**Our mission:** To help people feel spiritually present in sacred places by delivering their prayers with reverence, authenticity, and care, and by reminding them that they are seen, heard, and not alone.

---

## The Problem We Solve

Believers who long to place their prayers at holy sites in Jerusalem but are unable to be there physically. They want their prayers to feel personal and meaningful, not generic — and they're often skeptical of online religious services.

**Before us:**
- Feeling spiritually distant from holy places
- Inability to travel to Jerusalem
- Prayers feel impersonal or out of reach
- Skepticism toward online religious services

**After us:**
- A sense of spiritual presence at sacred places
- Prayers placed with care by real people at real locations
- Confirmation and closure — knowing their prayer was delivered
- Feeling seen, supported, and spiritually connected

---

## Our Unique Approach

What makes us different from alternatives:

1. **Faith meets geography and technology** — We bridge the physical gap between believers and holy places through a tangible, respectful, and documented experience.
2. **Real people, real locations, real delivery** — Every prayer is physically placed at a holy site by real people, with proof of placement.
3. **Sacred, not transactional** — Every prayer is treated as sacred. We serve people in moments of vulnerability, hope, and gratitude — never exploiting fear or guilt.
