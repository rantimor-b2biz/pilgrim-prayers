# Weekly Team Standup

**Meeting:** Every Sunday at 9:00 AM
**Duration:** 60-90 minutes
**Led by:** Agency Manager (Gatekeeper Agent)
**Attendees:** All agents + Project Lead

---

## Meeting Purpose

- **Status Review:** Where we stand on current week's deliverables
- **Performance Analysis:** Metrics from published content
- **Strategic Recommendations:** What to focus on next week
- **Action Planning:** Clear assignments and deadlines
- **Coordination:** Ensure all agents aligned and on track

---

## Meeting Structure (90 minutes)

### PART 1: PERFORMANCE REVIEW (20 minutes)

**Agency Manager presents:**
- Blog post performance (views, time on page, shares)
- Newsletter metrics (open rate, click rate, conversions)
- Video engagement (watch time, completion rate)
- Prayer placements (volume, trends)
- Website traffic (sources, quality, conversion)
- Competitive landscape update (any threats or opportunities)

**Format:** Dashboard view with key metrics highlighted

---

### PART 2: CURRENT WEEK STATUS (15 minutes)

**Agency Manager reviews each agent's progress:**

| Agent | Deliverable Due | Status | Blocker? | On Track? |
|-------|-----------------|--------|----------|-----------|
| Research | Research brief | TBD | [ ] | [ ] |
| Storyteller | Prayer narrative | TBD | [ ] | [ ] |
| Copywriter | Blog post | TBD | [ ] | [ ] |
| Newsletter | Email campaign | TBD | [ ] | [ ] |
| Visual | Video direction | TBD | [ ] | [ ] |

**If blocker detected:** Immediately escalate and solve in real-time

---

### PART 3: STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATIONS (20 minutes)

**Agency Manager presents recommendations for next week:**

**What's Working (Double Down)**
- Post topic/format with highest engagement
- Best performing channel or CTA
- Most engaged audience segment
- Content type to replicate

**What Needs Attention (Fix or Pivot)**
- Low engagement areas
- Underperforming content types
- Missed opportunities
- Optimization opportunities

**New Opportunities (Explore)**
- Emerging trends in evangelical Christian space
- Competitor activity/gaps
- Audience requests/questions
- Seasonal/event opportunities
- Partnership opportunities

**Growth Recommendations (Next 30 days)**
- Platform expansion? (new social channels?)
- A/B testing opportunities? (headlines, CTAs, timing?)
- Content calendar adjustments?
- Landing page improvements?
- Sales/conversion optimization?

---
