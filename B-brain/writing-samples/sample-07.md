# Israel Support Email — Crisis Campaign

**Platform:** Email
**Date:** 2026-03-01
**Type:** Conversion email — special send
**Subject:** Pray for the Peace of Jerusalem — Today

---

These are difficult days in Israel.

When the Holy Land trembles, believers around the world feel it too.

This is not a political moment. It is a spiritual one.

Scripture calls us clearly:

"Pray for the peace of Jerusalem: may those who love you be secure." — Psalm 122:6

Today, that prayer carries deep meaning.

Our team remains here in the Holy Land, near the sacred places where Jesus walked and taught. Even in uncertain days, we continue to carry prayers with reverence and faith.

If your heart feels stirred, stand with Jerusalem in prayer today.

Place a prayer for:

- Protection
- Peace
- Comfort for families
- Strength for the people of Israel

From your heart to God's ear.

With love and faith,
The Pilgrim Prayers Team

[Place a Prayer for Israel Today]

---

**Why this works:**
- Opens with a spiritual observation, not a news headline — keeps the framing sacred, not political
- Psalm 122:6 as the anchor — direct, recognizable, creates spiritual obligation
- "This is not a political moment. It is a spiritual one." — redirects the reader from news mode to faith mode in one sentence
- CTA language is active: "stand with Jerusalem in prayer today" — not passive ("if you feel moved")
- List of prayer intentions gives the reader something concrete to hold
- "From your heart to God's ear." — the brand's signature closing line
- Short. Every sentence earns its place. Nothing is decorative.
