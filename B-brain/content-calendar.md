# Pilgrim Prayers — 2026 Content Calendar

> A year of weekly stories from Jerusalem and the Holy Land, aligned to the Evangelical Christian calendar.

---

## Calendar Philosophy

This calendar is built for **Evangelical Christians** — our primary audience. It focuses on:

- **Core Christological events:** Christmas, Good Friday, Easter, Ascension, Pentecost
- **Scripture-rooted themes:** Psalms, Gospels, Acts — not liturgical traditions
- **Seasons in the Holy Land:** What Jerusalem looks and feels like throughout the year
- **The 6 rotating post structures** from `T-tools/skills/weekly-post-skill/`
- **No Catholic-specific observances:** No saints' feast days, Marian devotions, Corpus Christi, or Holy Days of Obligation

### What We Include
- Easter, Good Friday, Christmas, Pentecost, Ascension
- National Day of Prayer (US — first Thursday of May)
- Reformation Day (October 31) — light touch
- Biblical themes tied to seasons and places
- Seasons in Jerusalem and the Holy Land

### What We Skip
- Ash Wednesday / Lent (most Evangelicals don't observe formally)
- All Saints' Day (Catholic emphasis)
- Advent as a formal liturgical season (we cover Christmas preparation naturally)
- Saints' feast days, Marian feasts, Corpus Christi
- Any Catholic Holy Days of Obligation

## How to Use This Calendar

1. **Each Monday:** Check which week you're in and what the topic is
2. **Storyteller Agent:** Uses the topic, structure, and site to draft the post
3. **Gatekeeper:** Reviews against brand voice and calendar alignment
4. **Newsletter Agent:** Adapts the approved post for email
5. **After publishing:** Update `M-memory/learning-log.md` with what worked

---

## Flexibility

This calendar is a guide, not a prison. Adjust when:
- A real story from the field takes priority (always prefer real over planned)
- Current events in Jerusalem create a timely moment
- Audience feedback points to a topic they want more of
- A special prayer placement creates a powerful story opportunity
- The **Research Agent's monthly trend report** identifies a more timely or resonant topic
- A **Content Opportunity Alert** from the Research Agent flags something worth swapping in

The Research Agent provides data-backed calendar recommendations in `B-brain/monthly-trends/[YYYY-MM]-trend-report.md` every month. Review these to decide whether to swap, add, or reprioritize upcoming weeks.

When adjusting, keep the 6-structure rotation roughly balanced.
